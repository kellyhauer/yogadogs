Yoga is Amazing!

Yoga is essential to a calm, happy life! It is the base of a mindful meditation practice. Although yoga is calming, it is also fun -- check out this blog by Yoga Dork, who makes yoga fun and inviting for anyone.

Some benefits of a yoga practice include:

  • lower stress
  • improved flexibility
  • improved muscle strength
  • protects your spine
  • boosts your immunity
  • helps you sleep better

    • See! Anyone can do yoga!

    Types of Yoga

    Type Description
    Hatha Basic yoga, best for beginners
    Bikram Hot yoga, 95-100 degrees F
    Ashtanga Free flowing, physically demanding
    Vinyasa Focus on flow, emphasises sun salutation
    Kundalini Root chakra, spiritual - includes chants and meditations
    Iyengar Uses props, focuses on proper alignment
    Anusara Opens the heart, most spiritual of all
    Restorative Yoga for lazy people, simple poses and props
